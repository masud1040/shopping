Which iPhone Should I Get?

If you have an iPhone that you’re happy with, keep it. We don’t think you should upgrade just because Apple has released new phones. But if your current phone is running too slowly or is damaged, or if you’re simply ready for an upgrade and you want a new phone now, we recommend the Apple iPhone 16. It offers an almost Pro-like iPhone experience, with new shortcut buttons, excellent cameras, lengthy battery life, and a powerful processor. Just don’t expect artificial intelligence—yet.
